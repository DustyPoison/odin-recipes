Enjoy Latkes with this great recipe!

Whether you celebrate Christmas, Hanukkah, or any other winter time celebration, latkes are always a delicious winter time treat! Here we are going to help you make the tastiest latkes this season. With only a little time, love, and prep tastiness is 30 minutes away!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of shredded potato
  • 1/4 cup of grated onions
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 6 crushed saltine crackers
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix your potato, onion, beaten eggs, crushed saltines, salt and pepper into a large bowl.
  2. Pour 1/4 inch of oil in a heavy skillet and heat over medium high heat level.
  3. Scoop out a spoonful of the potato mix, and drop into the oil. Make sure to remove excess liquid into the bowl before placing on the skillet. Slightly flatten the potato mix with the back of your spoon once in the skillet. Make sure thickness is even.
  4. Cook until browned and crisp, between 3-5 minutes each side. Place onto a paper towel lined plate and allow oil to drain off.
  5. Serve with a dollop of applesauce on top. Enjoy!
