A Jewish donut without the hole: Sufganiyot

What is better than a donut? A donut without a hole! This Jewish recipe is certain to make your mouth drool this holiday season. In this article we are going to take you step by step to satiate your hunger.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 cups of self rising flour
  • 16 ounces of yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of vanilla sugar
  • 3 quarts of veggie oil
  • 1 pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, eggs, yogurt, sugar, salt and vanilla sugar in a mixing bowl. After mixing well, set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. After 30 minutes, take the dough and make doughballs 2 inches in diameter.
  3. Heat your veggie oil in a large pot to 365 degrees Farenhiet. Use a slotted spoon to deep fry the sufganiyot until golden and crispy. Set the sufganiyot to the side to cool.
  4. Sprinkle some powdered sugar over and enjoy!
